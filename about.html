About Our Company

Building a legacy of excellence, innovation, and trust in South Africa's construction and project management industry

Our Story

Shauny and Donny Trading and Projects (Pty) Ltd is a dynamic and versatile private company registered in South Africa (Registration Number: 2025/511366/07). Founded in June 2025, we operate from our headquarters at 1 Grobbler Street, Westerburg, Polokwane, Limpopo.

As a B-BBEE Exempted Micro Enterprise, we are committed to contributing to South Africa's economic transformation while delivering exceptional services to our clients. Our business activities are not restricted, allowing us to adapt and excel across multiple sectors including construction, maintenance, cleaning services, and comprehensive project management.

Under the leadership of Director Thévie Cynthia Gngbu, our team brings professional expertise and dedication to every project. We pride ourselves on our compliance with South African business regulations and our commitment to ethical business practices.

With our financial year ending in November, we maintain transparent financial practices and are positioned for sustainable growth and long-term partnerships with our clients across South Africa.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction and project management services that exceed client expectations while contributing positively to South Africa's economic development.

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of integrated trading and project solutions in South Africa, recognized for quality, innovation, and transformative impact.

Our Values

Integrity, Quality, Innovation, Customer Focus, and Commitment to Economic Transformation through B-BBEE compliance.
